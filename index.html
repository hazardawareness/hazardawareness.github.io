About Me

Well that title wasn't very descriptive so I'll go into a little more detail here. I'm a graphic designer who has just moved from London to Perth as the lifestyle suits me so much more here! With 5 years graphic design experience in both print and digital, I'm looking for the next challenge where I can further develop my design skills and carry on in the profession that I love.

I love my sports and everything outdoors so when I'm not sitting inside designing on my mac, I'm outside training, competing and having fun with sports both water and land based.

If you're looking for a full time designer in Perth, check out some of my work below and get in touch. I'm sure I can be a great assset to your company.
